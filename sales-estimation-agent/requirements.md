# E-Commerce Mobile App Requirements

## Overview
We are looking to develop a new mobile application for our clothing brand, "Urban Threads". The primary goal is to provide a seamless shopping experience for our existing customer base and attract new mobile-first shoppers.

## Supported Platforms
- iOS (iPhone)
- Android

## Core Features
1. **Product Catalog**
   - High-resolution image galleries
   - Advanced filtering (Size, Color, Price, Style)
   - "Out of stock" indicator

2. **User Accounts**
   - Social login (Google, Apple)
   - Order history and tracking
   - Wishlist functionality

3. **Shopping Cart & Checkout**
   - Multiple payment gateways (Credit Card, UPI, Apple Pay, Google Pay)
   - Address management
   - Promo code support

4. **Additional Requirements**
   - Push notifications for new arrivals and sales
   - Admin dashboard to manage inventory and view orders

## Project Scope & Budget
- **Timeline:** We need the MVP ready within 8-10 weeks.
- **Scale:** We expect around 5,000 active users in the first month and have an initial catalog of roughly 200 items. 
- **Budget:** We are flexible but are looking for a realistic estimate based on the features listed above.

## Questions for the Agency
- Can you integrate with our existing Shopify backend?
- Do you provide post-launch maintenance, and how much does it cost?
